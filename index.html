WhoWe Are!

Tiftie, one of the largest apparel wholesale and export company with over 10+ countries and 50,000+ global B2B customer; dealing since 2020 from Kolkata, India.
India's first wholesale marketplace with over a million products in 10+ segments; men, women & kids wear and much more clothing, all under one roof. We strive to make quality products and offer wide range of ideal quality at lucrative value.
Significant endeavor of Tiftie; Introducing the self-reliant women. 10,000+ women are now self-reliant and prolonging the wholesale chain.

Why We Rock!

Quality

Quality Product is an experience of Tiftie's customer. It's very vital for apparel materials. Reliability comes from achieving the quality standard and we're always careful to maintain the quality of the product that is really a important matter to us.

Price

Customers want value for money and we always tries to provide value worthy product. Prices are set according to the monetary condition of the customer.

Innovation

Tiftie is an another name of innovation. It is the successful exploitation of the creation, development and implementation of newish apparel.

Performance

Performance, which is closely tied to commercial effectiveness. Tiftie is determined to give their best to the customer. We always send our upbeat service.

Commitment

The definition of a commitment is a promise or agreement. Tiftie's commitment involves a long-term desire to maintain a valued partnership of both customer and trader.

Support

A seller is responsible making the selling process easy for customer. Tiftie is an ideal example who send the upbeat and attentive service for continuing their business.
